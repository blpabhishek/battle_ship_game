Welcome to the battleship game help option

It is a two player game and can be played on two different computers. Each player have five ships and two grids, first ocean grid where he can place his ships and second one is target grid where he can call-out a shoot to hit opponent's ship. Each ship has fixed size and to sunk that ship player has to attack on that ship as many time as size of that ship. Who will destroy all of his opponent ship will be the winner of the game. List of ship involved in this game.
Name Size
Battleship 4
Cruiser 3
Carrier 5
Destroyer 2
Submarine 3

*Please go through the folowing instructioin if player are playing this game first time.

  1. For placing a ship, player have to click on any box within the ocean-grid.

  2. The player can place the ships horizontally or vertically.

  3. The positions where ships are placed will be in red color.

  4. The player has to say ready as soon as deployed all the ships. This ready button will appear after deployment of all ship.

  5. After saying ready, player will get the target grid.

  6. Player has to wait till the opponent player is ready.

  7. Turn will be displayed on the screen. According to that player can shoot on target grid.

  8. If player locates the opponent's ship correctly, then it will be a hit; otherwise it will be a miss. Hit and miss will be represented in red and green respectively.

  9. Player will get alternative turns

    10. Victory

    The player who successfully locates all the opponent's ships first by hitting each square they occupy is the winner as all ships have been destroyed.
