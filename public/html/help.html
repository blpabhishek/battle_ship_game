BATTLESHIP GAME
Help Section

It is a two player game and can be played on two different computers. Each player has five ships and two grids, first ocean grid where he can place his ships and the second one is target grid where he can call out a shoot to hit opponent's ship. Each ship has fixed size and to sunk that ship player has to attack that ship as many time as the size of that ship. Who will destroy all of his opponent ship will be the winner of the game. List of ships involved in this game.

Name Size
Battleship 4
Cruiser 3
Carrier 5
Destroyer 2
Submarine 3

Please go through the following instructions if you are playing this game for the first time.


  1. For placing a ship, you have to click on any box within the ocean grid.

  2. You can place the ships horizontally or vertically.

  3. The positions where ships are placed will be in green color.

  4. You have to say ready as soon as you have deployed all the ships.Ready button will appear after deployment of all ships.

  5. After saying ready, you will get the target grid.

  6. You have to wait until your opponent is not ready.

  7. Turn message will be displayed on the screen. According to that, you can shoot on target grid.

  8. If you locate the opponent's ship correctly, then it will be a hit otherwise, it will be a miss. Hit and miss will be represented in red and green respectively.

  9. Each player will get alternative turns.

Victory:Who will destroy all of his opponent ship first will be the winner of the game.
